Bali


Bali is a beautiful island in Indonesia known for its beaches, temples, culture, and natural beauty. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world.

  1. Uluwatu Temple - A famous cliffside sea temple.
  2. Tanah Lot Temple - A scenic temple surrounded by ocean.
  3. Ubud Rice Terraces - Stunning green landscapes.
  4. Mount Batur - Famous for sunrise trekking.
  5. Kuta Beach - Popular for surfing and nightlife.
  • Nasi Goreng - Traditional Indonesian fried rice.
  • Babi Guling - Famous Balinese roasted pork.
  • Satay - Grilled meat skewers with sauce.
  • Mie Goreng - Indonesian fried noodles.
  • Lawar - Traditional mixed vegetable dish.
