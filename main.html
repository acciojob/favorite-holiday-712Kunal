Bali


Bali is a beautiful island in Indonesia known for its beaches, temples, culture, and natural beauty.

  1. Uluwatu Temple is famous for its cliffside views.
  2. Tanah Lot Temple offers stunning sunset scenery.
  3. Ubud Rice Terraces are breathtaking and iconic.
  4. Mount Batur is popular for sunrise hikes.
  5. Kuta Beach is known for its vibrant nightlife.
